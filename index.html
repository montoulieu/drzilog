Bio

Dr. Zilog was conceived by Pieter Montoulieu in 2007 due to a yearning to blend heavy metal, prog rock, weird samples, and chiptune music.

After years of writing music as a solo project, DZ recently brought on Jackson Kauman as a second guitarist. Jackson also assisted with riffs, chiptune melodies and drums on the latest Dr. Zilog album, Obstreperous.
